Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

This is a recipe for a grilled cheese sandwich. Best served with tomato soup.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Your choice of cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Apply butter to one side of each slice of bread
  2. Assemble sandwich
  3. Heat on griddle or skillet until cheese is melted and bread is golden brown
  4. Enjoy!
