Breakfast Burrito

Description

This is a recipe for a breakfast burrito. Breakfast burritos are yummy.

Ingredients

  • Flour Tortilla
  • Eggs
  • Breakfast Sausage
  • Cheese
  • Hot Sauce

Steps

  2. Scramble eggs and cook
  3. Heat up breakfast sausage
  4. Mix sausage and eggs together
  5. add eggs, sausage, cheese and desired hot sauce to tortilla and wrap
  6. Enjoy!
